# Universal Resume Template

## Target Role Profile
**Role**: Dynamic based on user prompt (e.g., Platform Engineer, Healthcare Administrator, Marketing Manager)  
**Industry**: Dynamic based on target role (e.g., Technology, Healthcare, Finance, Government)  
**Experience Level**: Dynamic based on years of experience (0-3, 3-8, 8+ years)  
**Key Focus Areas**: Determined by role type and industry requirements

## Content Selection Rules

### Universal Selection Criteria
- **Relevance**: Match experience content to target role keywords and requirements
- **Recency**: Prioritize recent experience (last 5-10 years) over older roles
- **Impact**: Emphasize quantified achievements and measurable business impact
- **Industry alignment**: Select content that demonstrates relevant industry knowledge

### Dynamic Tag Matching
Kiro will automatically identify and prioritize tags based on target role:
- **Technical roles**: "automation", "systems", "performance", "scalability", "security"
- **Management roles**: "leadership", "team-building", "strategy", "budget", "growth"
- **Healthcare roles**: "patient-care", "compliance", "safety", "clinical", "regulatory"
- **Finance roles**: "financial-analysis", "risk-management", "compliance", "reporting"
- **Sales/Marketing roles**: "revenue", "customer-acquisition", "campaigns", "growth"

### Content Prioritization Framework
1. **High Priority**: Current role and most recent 2-3 positions
2. **Medium Priority**: Relevant previous experience that demonstrates career progression
3. **Low Priority**: Early career roles (unless specifically relevant)
4. **Always Include**: Relevant certifications, education, and key projects
5. **Emphasize**: Achievements that align with target role success metrics

## Section Configuration

### Professional Summary
Select appropriate summary variant from profile/summary.md based on target role:
- **Technical roles**: Technical Leadership or Individual Contributor summary
- **Management roles**: Management or Technical Leadership summary
- **Career transitions**: Career Transition summary
- **General applications**: General summary with role-specific adaptation

### Professional Experience
- **Maximum Roles**: 3-5 roles depending on experience level and relevance
- **Detail Level**: Current role gets 4-6 bullets, previous roles get 2-4 bullets
- **Focus Areas**: Adapt based on target role (technical depth, business impact, leadership, etc.)
- **Metrics Emphasis**: Industry-appropriate metrics (uptime, revenue, patient outcomes, etc.)

### Key Projects Section
- Include 2-4 most relevant projects that demonstrate target role capabilities
- Prioritize projects with quantified business impact
- Emphasize technical complexity for technical roles, business impact for management roles
- Include cross-functional collaboration for leadership positions

### Technical/Professional Skills
Group skills by relevance to target role:
- **Technical roles**: Technical skills first, then soft skills
- **Management roles**: Leadership skills first, then technical competencies
- **Healthcare roles**: Clinical skills, compliance knowledge, technology proficiency
- **Finance roles**: Financial analysis, regulatory knowledge, systems expertise
- **Sales/Marketing roles**: CRM systems, analytics tools, communication skills

### Education
- **Recent graduates**: Detailed education section with relevant coursework
- **Experienced professionals**: Brief mention focusing on relevant degrees/certifications
- **Career changers**: Emphasize transferable education and continuous learning

### Certifications
- Prioritize certifications most relevant to target role and industry
- Include expiration dates for time-sensitive certifications
- Group by relevance (professional, technical, industry-specific)

## Length and Emphasis Guidelines

### Page Allocation (Dynamic based on experience)
**1 Page (0-3 years experience)**:
- Contact + Summary: 15%
- Experience: 50%
- Education: 20%
- Skills: 10%
- Certifications: 5%

**2 Pages (3+ years experience)**:
- **Page 1**: Contact, Summary, Current Role, 1-2 Previous Roles
- **Page 2**: Additional Experience, Key Projects, Skills, Education, Certifications

### Bullet Point Allocation
- **Current Role**: 4-6 bullets (emphasize recent achievements)
- **Previous Roles**: 2-4 bullets each (focus on most relevant accomplishments)
- **Projects**: 2-3 bullets each (context, solution, impact)

### Industry-Specific Emphasis Areas
- **Technology**: System scale, performance metrics, automation impact, cost optimization
- **Healthcare**: Patient outcomes, safety improvements, compliance metrics, efficiency gains
- **Finance**: Financial impact, risk reduction, regulatory compliance, process improvements
- **Sales/Marketing**: Revenue generation, customer acquisition, campaign performance, market growth
- **Government**: Compliance adherence, process improvement, cost savings, citizen impact

## Language and Tone Adjustments

### Industry-Appropriate Terminology
- **Technology**: "Infrastructure as Code", "DevOps", "Agile", "CI/CD", "Observability"
- **Healthcare**: "Patient-centered care", "Clinical outcomes", "HIPAA compliance", "Quality metrics"
- **Finance**: "Risk management", "Regulatory compliance", "Financial analysis", "ROI"
- **Government**: "Public service", "Regulatory compliance", "Process improvement", "Stakeholder engagement"

### Technical Depth Adaptation
- **Technical audiences**: Include specific technologies, architectures, and methodologies
- **Business audiences**: Focus on business impact, ROI, and strategic outcomes
- **Mixed audiences**: Balance technical competency with business value demonstration

### Quantification Standards by Industry
- **Technology**: Uptime percentages, performance improvements, cost savings, deployment frequency
- **Healthcare**: Patient satisfaction scores, safety improvements, compliance rates, efficiency metrics
- **Finance**: Revenue impact, cost reductions, risk mitigation, accuracy improvements
- **Sales/Marketing**: Revenue growth, conversion rates, customer acquisition costs, market share
